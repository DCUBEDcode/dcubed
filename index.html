What it's like working with DCUBED

Do design a little different

What you won’t find us doing is spending weeks perfecting high quality design mocks.

Making such serious investment and commitment to a design so early on is a flawed way to work. Instead, expect from us enough visual documentation to help convey an idea. Once we all feel like it’s the right choice. We start bringing the idea to life with code.

Watch your app grow

Using Heroku, we are able to create and host any kind of app in seconds. This means that from Day 1 you have access to your application. You can now test whenever releases are made.

Get amongst it (or don't)

We create an environment of transparency and sharing so that you can see as much or as little as you want about the progress of your application.

Upon kick-off you will receive an invite to your projects channel in the communication app Slack. In here you will see all ‘commits’ made to the code repository, the moderation of the Trello project board and any commentary made by the development team.
